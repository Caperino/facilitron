What we do

There are countless enterprise management and IT support systems available on the market, but they often offer way more functionality than actually needed by small companies. As a result, businesses are charged for unused services but do not have an alternative. The general scope of this project is to provide the customer with a web application acting as an IT governance and support system built specifically for small businesses with reduced functionality and cost. This shall be achieved by focusing on widely used or needed features of such systems rather than specific cases.
